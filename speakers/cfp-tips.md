# Tips to get your talk accepted

All conferences have a Call for Papers (CFP). Writing a good proposal is paramount to getting accepted. Each conference is different and therefore the CFP evaluation process are also different. A convincing abstract, professional head-shot, and a impressive bio will surely improve your chances of getting through. Below are some tips that we think you will find useful before submitting
